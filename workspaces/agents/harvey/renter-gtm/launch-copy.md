## FAQ Copy

**Q: What is Renter?**
A: Renter is an all-in-one tenant platform that helps renters budget, apply, document their lease, learn their rights, and communicate with landlords.

**Q: How does the deposit alternative work?**
A: Instead of paying a large cash security deposit, eligible renters pay a smaller enrollment fee and Renter provides deposit coverage accepted by participating property managers.

**Q: Is Renter available in my city?**
A: We are launching in [launch cities] and expanding quickly. Join our waitlist to be notified when Renter comes to your market.

**Q: Does Renter replace my lease?**
A: No. Renter complements your lease by helping you organize documents, track communications, and understand your rights. Always review your actual lease carefully.

**Q: Is Renter free for renters?**
A: Renter offers free tools and guides. Some premium services, such as deposit alternatives, may include fees. We are transparent about costs upfront.
