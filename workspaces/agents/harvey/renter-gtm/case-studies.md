# Renter Case Studies — Launch Edition

## Case Study Template A: The Deposit-Free Move-In

### Title
“How Maya Cut Move-In Costs by 80% and Moved into Her Dream Apartment in 72 Hours”

### Customer Profile
- **Name:** Maya Okafor (pseudonym for privacy)
- **Segment:** Young professional, first-time solo renter
- **Location:** Urban market, launch city
- **Challenge:** Saved $4,200 for a new apartment but was asked for first month’s rent + security deposit + broker fee totaling $5,400 upfront.

### Situation
Maya found a great unit in a competitive neighborhood. The upfront cash requirement would have drained her emergency fund and forced her to borrow from family. She needed a fast, reputable way to secure the apartment without paying a traditional cash deposit.

### Solution
Maya used Renter’s deposit-alternative option: a small, refundable enrollment fee plus Renter-backed deposit coverage accepted by her property manager. Her application was verified in minutes through Renter’s reusable renter profile.

### Results
- **80% reduction** in move-in cash outlay ($4,320 saved day one)
- **72 hours** from search to signed lease
- **Zero** family loans or credit-card debt
- Application accepted ahead of two competing applicants because verification was instant and complete

### Quote
“Renter turned what felt impossible into the easiest move of my life. I kept my savings intact and still got the apartment I wanted.”

### Use in GTM
- Pillar: Financial Empowerment
- Formats: 60-second video testimonial, blog post, email nurture CTA, sales-deck slide, paid-social carousel
- Target KPIs: Deposit-alternative sign-ups; calculator completions

## Case Study Template B: The Transparent Landlord Relationship

### Title
“How Cedar Heights Reduced Tenant Disputes by 40% and Improved Lease Renewals”

### Customer Profile
- **Company:** Cedar Heights Property Management (pseudonym)
- **Segment:** Mid-size residential property manager, ~800 units
- **Location:** Mixed urban/suburban portfolio
- **Challenge:** Maintenance requests were scattered across email, texts, and phone calls; disputes over deposits and repair responsibility consumed staff time and hurt tenant satisfaction.

### Situation
Cedar Heights struggled with inconsistent documentation, delayed maintenance response times, and a 22% annual turnover rate. Disputes frequently escalated to mediation because neither side had clear records.

### Solution
Cedar Heights partnered with Renter to centralize maintenance requests, lease communications, and payment records in a shared, timestamped platform accessible to both tenants and staff.

### Results
- **40% reduction** in formal deposit/repair disputes within 6 months
- **18% improvement** in maintenance first-response time
- **12% increase** in lease renewals
- **$38,000 estimated annual savings** in administrative and mediation costs

### Quote
“Renter gives us the paper trail we always wished we had. Our residents feel heard, and our team spends less time arguing and more time fixing problems.”

### Use in GTM
- Pillar: Trust Between Renters & Landlords
- Formats: B2B one-pager, LinkedIn article, webinar slide, partner sales deck, trade-publication pitch
- Target KPIs: B2B qualified leads; partner co-branded engagement

## Case Study Template C: The Rights-Ready Renter

### Title
“How Jamal Recovered His Full Security Deposit Using Renter’s Move-Out Toolkit”

### Customer Profile
- **Name:** Jamal Rivera (pseudonym)
- **Segment:** Mid-career renter, moving cross-city for a new job
- **Location:** Launch market
- **Challenge:** Previous landlord withheld $1,800 of a $2,200 deposit for vague “cleaning and damages” without itemized documentation.

### Situation
Jamal suspected the deposit withholding was improper but did not have photos or records from move-in. He wanted to avoid a repeat when moving to his next apartment.

### Solution
Jamal used Renter’s move-in/move-out inspection checklist, photo documentation feature, and automated timeline of communications. When his new lease ended, he submitted the same evidence package to request deposit return.

### Results
- **100% deposit returned** ($2,200) within the legal deadline
- **Zero** back-and-forth with landlord
- **2 hours** total time spent documenting condition at move-in and move-out
- Created a reusable evidence record for future rentals

### Quote
“Last time I felt powerless. This time I had proof. Renter made it easy to stand up for myself without being confrontational.”

### Use in GTM
- Pillar: Tenant Rights & Fair Housing Education
- Formats: Blog post, downloadable checklist lead magnet, email nurture story, social proof in sales deck
- Target KPIs: Rights-guide downloads; checklist conversion rate

## Interview Guide (for capturing real stories)

1. What was the hardest part of renting before you found Renter?
2. What specific feature or service made the biggest difference?
3. Can you share a concrete result (time saved, money saved, stress reduced)?
4. What would you tell someone considering Renter?
5. How has renting changed for you now compared to before?

Capture metrics whenever possible: dollar amounts, hours, percentages, and deadlines.
