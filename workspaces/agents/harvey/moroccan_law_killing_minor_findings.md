## Key Legal Principles

1. **Crime of Murder (القتل):** Defined in Section 7 of the Moroccan Penal Code (القانون الجنائي) under crimes and offenses against persons.
2. **Enhanced Penalties for Minor Victims:** A general rule under Moroccan law provides that if the victim is under the age of 18 (قاصر), penalties—including fines and prison terms—are enhanced compared to adult victims.
3. **Aggravating Circumstances:** When the victim is a minor, the offense is treated more severely, often doubling or significantly increasing the applicable sentence.
